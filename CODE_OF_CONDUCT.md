# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in AI Router a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity or expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, sexual identity or orientation, or socioeconomic status.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and specific.
- Welcome questions and differing technical perspectives.
- Focus criticism on ideas and code, not people.
- Respect privacy and do not publish another person's private information.
- Accept responsibility, apologize when appropriate, and learn from mistakes.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, sexualized content, sustained disruption, and other behavior that makes participation unsafe are not acceptable.

## Scope

This policy applies in repository issues, pull requests, reviews, discussions, release channels, and public spaces where someone represents the project.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainers using the contact method on their GitHub profile. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as practical. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior violates this policy.

This policy is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html), version 2.1.
